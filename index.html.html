Prioritization & Roadmap

RICE Framework Decision

Why Search First?

  • Reach: 100% of users (vs. 40% for messaging)
  • Impact: Direct effect on North Star metric
  • Confidence: 90% based on research
  • Effort: 1 sprint vs. 3-4 for alternatives

Phased Rollout

  • Phase 1 (3 weeks): Enhanced search + filters
